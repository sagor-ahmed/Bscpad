What is EvolvePad?

The Evolve Launch Pad is the first decentralized cross-chain platform for the benance Smart chain Network & Ethereum network.

Decentralized projects wil be able to raise and exchange capital cheap and first. Users wil be able to participate in a secure and compliant environment and to use assets that go away beyond the current ERC20 & BSC standard with cross chain Interoperability.

In order to leverage advantages smooth Interoperability between various network is a key to success. The Killer feature of Evolve pad is the possibility of making cross chain swaps,powered by the Binance Smart Chain Network, Which Can provide higher throughput for faster and cheaper transactions while staying connected to the Etheruem Network and other blockchains for liquitidy.
